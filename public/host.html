Corporate Mode (table hopping)

One switch for corporate gigs. When on: the waiting screen shows your company logo instead of the heart (and the heartbeat goes silent), the personal message cards and karaoke are skipped, and "Show Messages" goes straight to the thank-you / review screen below. Turn off to return to the normal private-party show. Auto-saves; no redeploy needed.
